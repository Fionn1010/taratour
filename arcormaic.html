Teach Cormaic

This page uses in-page WebXR AR placement. Scan the ground, wait for the ring to appear, then tap the screen to place the model. Once placed, it stays fixed so you can walk around it or inside it.

  1. Tap Start AR
  2. Move your phone slowly to detect the ground
  3. When the green ring appears, tap the screen to place the model
  4. Walk around the structure, or into it if the model allows
