Hill of Tara Tour

Explore one of Ireland’s most important ancient ceremonial landscapes through a guided mobile tour. Follow the route on your phone, move between the main points of interest, and unlock the Stone of Destiny mixed-reality stop as part of the experience.

How it works

Simple on-site experience

Open the tour on your phone, allow location access, and follow the route between the stops. As you move around the Hill of Tara, the tour can guide you by distance and direction.

Included

Guided storytelling

The experience is built around the main highlights of the site, with location-aware navigation and a dedicated Stone of Destiny MR page linked from the main tour flow.

Best on

Your mobile phone

This tour is designed for mobile browsers with GPS enabled. For the best experience, use it outdoors on site and allow camera access when launching the MR stop.

Before you begin

Quick tips

  • Use the tour outdoors at the Hill of Tara for accurate positioning.
  • Allow location access when prompted so the tour can guide you to the active stop.
  • When you reach the Stone of Destiny stop, open the MR view and allow camera access.
  • Headphones will improve the narration experience in windy conditions.
