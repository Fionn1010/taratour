The six stops

The timeline has been rebuilt around the story of a new High King: from arrival at Tara to final recognition in Teach Cormaic.

Stop 1

Entrance

The welcome begins and the visitor steps into the hill’s larger story.

Stop 2

Banqueting Hall

The king walks the long earthen avenue while the people gather to witness him.

Stop 3

Rath of the Synods

Within the sacred enclosure, blessing and legitimacy take shape.

Stop 4

Mound of the Hostages

The king pauses before the ancient dead and the long memory of the land.

Stop 5

Stone of Destiny

The central moment of transformation, where kingship is recognized.

Stop 6

Teach Cormaic

The journey closes in warmth and acknowledgement as the people accept the new king.
