# Hill of Tara Mobile Tour

A cinematic, location-based mobile experience that brings the Hill of Tara to life through video, sound, and storytelling.

---

## 🎬 Overview

This project is a **mobile-first web tour** designed to be experienced on-site at the Hill of Tara.

Visitors move through six key locations, each unlocking a cinematic scene with:

- 🎥 Fullscreen video
- 🎧 Ambient sound design
- 🎤 Narration
- 📍 Optional GPS-triggered playback

The experience follows the journey of a newly crowned High King.

---

## 🗺️ Tour Structure

1. **Entrance**  
   Arrival and introduction to the hill

2. **Banqueting Hall**  
   The king walks the great ceremonial avenue

3. **Rath of the Synods**  
   Sacred enclosure and ritual authority

4. **Mound of the Hostages**  
   Reflection with the ancestors

5. **Stone of Destiny**  
   The moment of transformation

6. **Teach Cormaic**  
   Recognition and acceptance
