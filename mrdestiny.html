About this experience

This Phase 1 page focuses on one hero stop only: the Stone of Destiny. It uses compass guidance, optional GPS, and camera alignment to help guests face the landmark before launching the immersive video scene.

One-stop MVP

This launch version is trimmed down to a single destination so you can test one strong visitor moment on-site.

Stone of Destiny scene

When the guest is facing the landmark, the experience unlocks and plays the Stone of Destiny video from your assets folder.

Direction-aware guidance

Compass, fallback bearing, and optional location work together to point the guest toward the correct landmark.

Status: Phase 1 MVP Mode: Stone of Destiny only
