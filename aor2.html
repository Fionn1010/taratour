Suggested entrance behaviour

Keep this stop simple. The visitor should understand three things immediately: they are in the right place, this is an AR experience, and the guide will direct them up the correct path.

  • Welcome video plays first.
  • Visitor taps Launch AR Guide.
  • The guide appears near the gate area and gestures up the hill.
  • A short voice line points them toward the Banqueting Hall.
