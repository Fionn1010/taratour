Scene setup

This page uses classic script-based Three.js rather than module imports, because that is often more reliable on iPhone Safari.

Model 1: ./assets/model/palisade.glb
Model 2: ./assets/model/kingmodel.glb
What to test:
1. Does the scene appear at all?
2. Are both models visible?
3. Does hiding one improve smoothness?
4. Can you drag to orbit around the scene?
Important:
If this still fails on iPhone, the next fallback is to merge both models into one GLB in Blender, because that is much easier for Safari to render than two separate files in a live Three.js scene.
